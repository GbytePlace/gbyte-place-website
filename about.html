About Gbyte Place

A trusted bridge between global businesses and African talent.

Gbyte Place Management Services is a global workforce management company headquartered in Nigeria.

Our story

Exceptional talent should never be limited by geography.

Gbyte Place began with the belief that talented professionals across Nigeria and Africa deserve access to global opportunities that recognize their value and reward their work fairly.

The business traces its roots to Gbyte Place Global Limited, established in 2017. In December 2023, it evolved into Gbyte Place Management Services with a clearer focus on remote workforce management and long-term partnership.

Our purpose

To be the trusted bridge connecting global businesses with exceptional African talent.

Our vision

To become the company global businesses recommend when they need dependable remote teams.
